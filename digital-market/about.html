About CampusCart

Supporting student entrepreneurs

CampusCart is a student-centric platform designed to empower local entrepreneurs, provide convenient campus services, and foster a thriving community of vendors and customers.

Whether you're looking for beauty services, handmade products, or other goods, our platform makes it easy to discover, connect, and transact with trusted vendors.

Our Mission

To create a thriving ecosystem for student entrepreneurs by providing a digital marketplace that connects them with the campus community.

Our Vision

To be the leading campus marketplace platform that fosters entrepreneurship, innovation, and economic growth within the community.
